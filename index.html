Our Story

Rooted in
Family,
Rooted in Faith

"Every bar of soap tells a story — of our ancestors, our land, our love."

— Ericka Goff, Founder

EGOFF Essentials was born from a deep love of family, heritage, and the healing power of nature. Founded by Ericka Goff, the brand is a living tribute to Jamaican botanical wisdom passed down through generations of the Goff-Caliste family.

What began as a personal calling to create clean, purposeful skincare has grown into a full family endeavor — united under the banner of EGOFF Enterprise L.L.C. with two wings: the EGOFF Foundation (community giving) and EGOFF Essentials (natural skincare).

Jamaica

Where Our Roots
Grow Deep

🇯🇲

Island Heritage

Jamaica's lush landscape has gifted healers for centuries with aloe, turmeric, moringa, and herbs that modern science now confirms as powerhouses of wellness.

👩🏾

Women's Wisdom

Jamaican women have long been the keepers of botanical knowledge — mixing, brewing, and crafting remedies passed mother to daughter through the generations. Ericka carries this torch.

🌿

Botanical Legacy

From the Blue Mountains to the coast, Jamaica's biodiversity is unmatched. We honor this gift by sourcing thoughtfully, giving back through our Foundation, and letting the island's spirit guide every formula.
