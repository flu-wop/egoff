Our Story

Rooted in Family,
Rooted in Faith

EGOFF Essentials was born from a deep love of family, heritage, and the healing power of nature. Founded by Ericka Goff, the brand is a living tribute to Jamaican botanical wisdom passed down through generations of the Goff-Caliste family tree.

United under EGOFF Enterprise L.L.C., with two branches: the EGOFF Foundation for community giving, and EGOFF Essentials for natural skincare rooted in love.
