Jamaica · New Orleans · Faith

I was born and raised in New Orleans, Louisiana — a city of culture, spirit, and deep roots. But my soul stretches far beyond the bayou. My family originates from Wait-A-Bit, Trelawny, Jamaica — a place whose name alone carries the patience and wisdom of my ancestors.

My journey into soap-making began in the church. I was raised in faith, and it is through that same spirit of service and love that EGOFF Essentials was born. I started experimenting with natural ingredients, learning to honor the skin the way scripture honors the body — as something sacred.

What began as a gift for family and friends grew into a calling. I discovered that natural soap — when crafted with intention, prayer, and the finest ingredients — could do more than cleanse. It could support comfort, help soothe the body, and bring a sense of peace after a long day's labor.

Every bar I create carries the legacy of the Powerful Women of God who came before me — my grandmothers, aunts, and mother — women who nurtured with their hands and led with their faith.

EGOFF Essentials is not just soap. It is alchemy — the transformation of earth's purest gifts into something that touches your skin and reaches your soul. My soaps do not rush to dissolve. They are made with substance, with texture and scent that remind you: you deserve the real thing.

"With love from my hands to your skin — essentially rooted, essentially you."
— Ericka A. Goff, Your Soap Alchemist

Frequently Asked Questions

Our soaps are crafted by hand using only natural, purposeful ingredients — no synthetic detergents, no harsh chemicals, no fillers. Where commercial soaps strip the skin's natural oils, ours nourish and protect. They are dense and long-lasting, crafted with substance and intention.

With proper care, typically 4–8 weeks with regular daily use. Allow your bar to fully dry between uses on a draining soap dish away from standing water. Many customers report bars lasting much longer when used as a daily ritual.

Our formulations are gentle by design — no artificial dyes, no synthetic fragrances, no harsh preservatives. If you have known allergies or skin conditions, please review ingredients for each bar or reach out before ordering. For external use only.
