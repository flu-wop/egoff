My Story

Egoff Essentials was born from a great source of pain, a deep love of family, heritage and the healing power of nature and prayer. As Founder & CEO Ericka A. Goff, the brand is a living tribute to my Jamaican family's botanical wisdom passed down through generations of the Goff-Caliste family tree.

I was born in New Orleans but my roots originated in Wait-A-Bit, Trelawny, Jamaica and what we call the dirty south (New Orleans, Louisiana). I am named after my mother's best friend who died decades ago per her request.

Reared in the church — Corpus Christi, Hartzell United MC (Lower 9th Ward), Greater Saint Stephens, Franklin Avenue BC & currently Light House Church & Ministries, Humble TX — at a very young age is when my relationship with God evolved. My life has never been the same after accepting Christ. Prayer is always my comfort and solitude to keep striving.

Every bar is created for you — to embody, cleanse, embrace and to refresh one's sensual elements. Whether you're discovering your body's needs and scents for subtlety through texture and scent — we strive to understand and personalize every bar with grace, love, honor, earth's elements, all natural ingredients, skill and craftsmanship.

My/Our mission is to dig deep internally, connect fully with nature and respond and respect humanness continuously. So your selection will allow us to get a sense of your needs and preferences as you select from our curated collection.

It is my honor and mission to help and provide you with and find a soap that tells your unique story and suits your current and future needs.
